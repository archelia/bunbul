About Sinful

Sinful is a fashion brand company which is one of subsidiary of Viore Company (since 2000). Sinful was founded in November 2014. Located in Gading Griya Lestari Blok D1 Kav no 18 - 20, Jakarta Utara, DKI Jakarta, Indonesia, Sinful provides a wide range of women's fashion needs including shoes, bags, clothing and other fashion accessories. To maintain its customer satisfaction, Sinful only use the best materials for its products, which are comfortable to wear and still stay up to date with the fashion trends.
